About me!

I have been with KAT since the 2018 season. I started as a part-time sales rep, then advanced to full-time in 2019. I have been in United States for over 20 years, arriving as a child. I attended Bravo Middle School, graduated from John F. Kennedy High School in 2004, and took a University of Minnesota two year translation and interpreting planning program to work at the Court House. I reside in Columbia Heights for the past five years, married, and a father of three kids, I ride a motorcycle and enjoy fishing.
